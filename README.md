The Official ALCC Constitution
​Section I: The "Common Double Side" Rules
​These rules govern the use of a neutral/extra player used to balance out team numbers.
​Law 1 (Entry Timing): The Designated Common Double Side player is only permitted to enter the field and bat after the batting team is completely all out.
​Law 2 (Eligibility): The Common Double Side position must strictly be filled by a novice or "noob" player to ensure fair competition.
​Law 3 (Role Restriction): The Common Double Side player is strictly a batsman; they are completely barred from bowling.
​Section II: Gameplay & Ground Boundaries
​Law 4 (The Wide Pole Boundary): If a delivered ball strikes the wide pole at the edge and deflects outward, it is declared a Wide. If the ball strikes the pole and deflects inward (into the court/play area), it is Not a Wide.
​Law 5 (External Interference / Reball): If a batsman hits the ball and it strikes an external individual (any bystander or person not actively participating in the league), play is immediately halted and a Reball is called. No runs or wickets can count on that delivery.
​Law 6 (Seating & Crowd Control): Staff members will arrange seating strictly outside the court in the lawn area ground. All players not actively on the field, along with the audience, must remain in this designated zone.
​Section III: Reviews & Technology
​Law 7 (Umpire DRS Review): In the event of a close or confusing decision, the on-field umpires will consult the official review cameras to make the final determination.
​Law 8 (Team Review Quota): Each team is allocated exactly 2 reviews per innings.
​Section IV: Code of Conduct & Roster Integrity
​Law 9 (Umpire Dissent Penalty): Players are strictly prohibited from arguing with the umpire. Any instance of dissent or argument will result in an immediate 6-run penalty awarded to the opposing team.
​Law 10 (Roster Lock): Once a team is officially eliminated from the tournament, its players are locked. They are strictly barred from transferring to or playing for any other active teams in the league.
